Salmorejo Andaluz

Description

Salmorejo is a traditional cold soup from Córdoba, Andalusia. It is made with ripe tomatoes, bread, olive oil, and garlic, blended into a smooth and creamy dish. Served chilled and topped with hard-boiled egg and serrano ham, it is refreshing and full of Mediterranean flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg ripe tomatoes
  • 200 g stale bread
  • 150 ml (⅔ cup) extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs (for garnish)
  • 50 g serrano ham, diced (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the tomatoes. Blend them until smooth.
  2. Strain the tomato purée to remove seeds and skin.
  3. Add the bread, garlic, and salt. Blend again until smooth.
  4. Slowly add olive oil while blending to create a creamy emulsion.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  6. Serve cold, garnished with chopped hard-boiled egg and serrano ham.
