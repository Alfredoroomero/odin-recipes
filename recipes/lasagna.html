Traditional Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel, and plenty of cheese. Baked in the oven, it develops a soft and comforting texture inside with a golde, crispy top, making it hearty and flavorful meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 500g ground pork (optional, or use more beef)
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50 g (½ cup) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 250 g (2 cups) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 50 g butter
  • 50 g (½ cup) all-purpose flour
  • 750 ml (3 cups) milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in salted water until "al dente". Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil, add onion and garlic, and cook until soft.
  4. Add the ground beef (and pork if using) and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 20/30 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, make the béchamel sauce: melt butter in a saucepan, whisk in flour, then gradually add milk. Stir until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  7. Grease a baking dish. Start with a layer of meat sauce, then noodles, then béchamel, and sprinkle cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 35/40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
